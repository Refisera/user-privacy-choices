User Privacy Choice

At Refillmybottle app, we respect our users' privacy rights. As such, we offer you the ability to choose the level of privacy that you are most comfortable with. You can choose to:

Option 1: Allow use of location information

Allow Refillmybottle app to store and use your location information to provide water station locations.

Option 2: Opt-out of location information

Opt-out of allowing Refillmybottle app to store and use your location information. If you choose to opt-out, you will still be able to search for water station locations based on a specific location that you input.

Option 3: Request deletion of personal information

Ask Refillmybottle app to delete any personal information that we have collected from you.

Please email us at ilove@refillmybottle.com to communicate any privacy requests, questions, or concerns.
